About V. P. Kandhavel

Rooted in Sulur, focused on results — clean water, education, healthcare and sustainable development.

Vision

Every household in Sulur should have access to safe water, quality education, accessible healthcare, and dignified livelihoods.

Approach

  • Listen to people’s needs at ward-level meetings
  • Mobilize volunteers and experts to solve issues quickly
  • Work with local administrations transparently
  • Measure outcomes and publish updates
