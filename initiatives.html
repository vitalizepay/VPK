Initiatives

Key projects improving daily life across Sulur.

Water Restoration

Pipeline repair, handpump maintenance, tanker scheduling, and water testing to ensure safe access.

  • 120+ water points repaired
  • Ward-level maintenance rosters
  • Quality testing published monthly

Education Support

Scholarships and learning kits for students, with mentoring and career guidance workshops.

  • 35 merit scholarships
  • 1000+ notebooks distributed
  • Career camps in 8 schools

Health Camps

Periodic general and specialist camps with local hospitals; mobile clinic to remote hamlets.

  • 60+ free camps conducted
  • Blood donation & eye check-ups
  • Medicine distribution
